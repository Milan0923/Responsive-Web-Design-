Rajasthan is a State of India is situated in the wisest of northern India.
It is a land of bravery & chivalry, romance & royalty class.
It is a land of contrast having scorching desert on one hand
& high green conifers trees on the other hand. There are also places full of lakes & rivers.

This Marwari phrase "पधारो म्हारे देश" - “Padharo Mhare Desh”,
meaning “Welcome to my Land” not only stands as our welcome message for visitors
from all across the globe, but, also reveals hospitality of the people of Rajasthan.
